# Privacy Policy — Australian Citizenship Test App

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview
Australian Citizenship Test ("the app") is a study tool for the Australian citizenship test. This policy explains how your data is handled.

## Data Collection
This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no servers, and no analytics.

## Local Storage
All study progress (quiz results, streaks, starred questions) is stored **locally on your device only** using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services
This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.
